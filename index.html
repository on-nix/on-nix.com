On Nix

Everything you need to build reliable systems!


On Nix is a collection of projects which will help you modernize your development and production systems with Nix .

Our mission is to make you feel that getting things running on Nix is as easy as on any other technology, but at the same time with thousands of benefits that you won't find anywhere else.

Everything here is 100% Free and Open Source Software released into the Public Domain . You are encouraged to stand on our shoulders , use these projects everywhere, for any purpose, commercial or non-commercial, as an individual, at work, or in your organization.

We hope this software is useful to you, as it is useful to us and many other developers around the globe! ❤️
